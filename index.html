公司简介About Us

上海慧逸电气设计中心自2019年成立以来，始终专注于电力系统核心设备——变压器的运维技术服务。我们致力于为各类工矿企业、电力公司及大型用电单位提供涵盖变压器维护保养、技术升级改造、老旧设备性能恢复等在内的系统性解决方案。

依托资深的工程技术团队与长期行业经验，公司形成了一整套科学、高效、安全的服务流程，从现场勘查、问题诊断到定制化设计与施工执行，确保每一台设备稳定运行，延长其生命周期。

慧逸电气秉承“以专业赢信任、以技术创价值”的理念，注重服务质量与技术创新的同步提升。我们不仅具备ISO9001质量体系认证和多项行业技术资质，还与节能协会等专业机构保持长期合作，持续推动绿色节能与智能化电气改造服务。

未来，公司将进一步拓展服务广度与技术深度，推动变压器全生命周期管理服务体系建设，助力中国智能电网与可持续能源发展。

Since its establishment in 2019, Shanghai Huiyi Electric Design Center has focused on transformer operation and maintenance services as a core component of the power system. We provide systematic solutions covering maintenance, technical upgrades, and performance restoration of aging equipment for industrial, utility, and large-scale power users.

With a highly experienced engineering team and deep industry expertise, we deliver a full-service pipeline from on-site diagnosis to customized solution design and execution—ensuring safe, efficient, and long-lasting equipment performance.

Adhering to the principle of “Winning trust through professionalism, creating value through technology,” Huiyi emphasizes both service quality and technological innovation. We hold ISO9001 certification, various technical qualifications, and collaborate with energy conservation associations to advance sustainable, intelligent power solutions.

Looking ahead, we aim to expand our technical depth and service scope, contributing to the development of full life-cycle transformer management and China’s smart grid and sustainable energy transition.
